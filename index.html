    How to Use the Online Alarm Clock

    Using PremiClock Pro to set an alarm is quick and easy, ensuring you never miss an important event. Follow these simple steps for reliable online timekeeping:

    • **Select the Time:** Use the hour and minute input fields in the central card to specify the desired time. You can manually type the numbers or use the small arrow buttons.
    • **Choose AM/PM:** Ensure the AM or PM selector is correctly set for morning or evening alarms.
    • **Use Quick Set Panel:** Click any of the pre-set hour buttons on the left (under AM or PM) to quickly set the alarm hour to the top of that hour (e.g., '5 PM' sets the time to 05:00 PM) and instantly activate the alarm.
    • **Pick a Sound:** Use the "Choose Sound" dropdown menu to select your preferred audio alert, such as 'Classic Tone' or 'Morning Birds'.
    • **Loop Option:** Check the "Loop Sound" box if you want the alarm to continue ringing until you manually click "Stop Alarm."
    • **Activate:** Click the **Set Alarm** button. The status message below will confirm the exact time your alarm is set for and will be added to the "Recently Set" list.
    • **Important:** For the alarm to work, the browser tab must remain open and your device must be awake.

      No. For the online alarm clock to remain active and ring at the set time, the browser tab must remain open and your device must be awake (not in sleep or standby mode).

      If you set an alarm for a time that has already passed in the current day, the system automatically sets the alarm for the same time on the next day to ensure you receive the intended alert.
