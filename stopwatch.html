How to Use the Online Stopwatch

The PremiClock Pro Stopwatch is perfect for accurately measuring elapsed time in training, science experiments, or competitive events. It includes a Lap feature to track intermediate timings.

  • **Start/Stop:** Click the **Start** button (pink) to begin timing. The button changes to **Stop** while running. Click it again to pause the measurement.
  • **Lap Recording:** While the stopwatch is running, click the **Lap** button (blue) to record the current time in the **Lap History** section on the right. This lets you track intermediate times without stopping the main clock.
  • **Reset:** Click the **Reset** button (dark purple) to clear all recorded time and lap history, returning the stopwatch to 00:00:00. This button is only active when the stopwatch is stopped.
  • **Precision:** The stopwatch measures time up to hundredths of a second (0.01s) for high accuracy.
  • **Best Practice:** For accurate timing, use a modern browser and ensure the stopwatch tab remains open.
